# Refund Policy Update - No Partial Refund When Paused

## ✅ Changes Made

The refund policy has been updated to clearly state that **there are NO partial refunds under any circumstance**, including when campaigns are paused.

---

## 📋 Updated Messages

### 1. **Refund Policy Header**
**Before:**
> ❌ No Refunds Once Paid

**After:**
> ❌ No Refunds - Ever

**Content Updated:**
> "Once you submit payment, **no refunds will be issued**—regardless of reason, timing, or status. This includes when campaigns are paused or stopped."

---

### 2. **Pause Section Heading**
**Before:**
> ⏸️ Pause Anytime

**After:**
> ⏸️ Pause Anytime (No Refund)

**Content Updated:**
> "You can **pause** your campaign at any time and **resume it later**. **No partial refunds** when paused—full budget remains held."

---

### 3. **Agreement Checkbox**
**Before:**
> "I understand and accept the no refund policy. I can pause or stop my campaign anytime."

**After:**
> "I understand there are **no refunds under any circumstance**. Payment is non-refundable whether campaign is active, paused, or stopped."

---

### 4. **Important Note (Tip Section)**
**Before:**
> "Review your campaign details carefully before payment. Make sure everything is correct—you cannot edit once submitted for payment."

**After:**
> "Review your campaign details carefully before payment. Make sure everything is correct—you cannot edit once submitted for payment. **No refunds for any reason, including when paused.**"

---

### 5. **Pause Button Tooltip**
**Before:**
> "Pause campaign (can resume later)"

**After:**
> "Pause campaign (no refund, can resume later)"

---

## 🎯 What This Means for Users

| Action | Refund Amount |
|--------|---------------|
| Pause campaign after 50% spent | **$0 refund** - Full budget stays held |
| Pause campaign for 1 week then resume | **$0 refund** - Full budget stays held |
| Stop campaign with remaining budget | **$0 refund** - Full budget forfeited |
| Campaign expires naturally | **$0 refund** - Full budget used up |

---

## 📊 User Journey

```
Company pays SGD $500 for campaign
    ↓
Campaign runs for 2 weeks
    ↓
Budget spent: $320 (64%)
Remaining budget: $180
    ↓
User clicks "Pause" button
    ↓
Campaign pauses
    ↓
💰 Refund issued: $0
💾 Budget held: $500 (full amount)
    ↓
User can click "Resume" to continue
    ↓
Campaign resumes
    ↓
Remaining $180 continues to be spent
```

## 🔐 Legal/Business Protection

By clearly stating **NO refunds under any circumstance**, the platform:
✅ Protects against fraudulent charge reversals
✅ Prevents gaming the pause feature for refunds
✅ Clearly sets expectations upfront
✅ Requires explicit user agreement
✅ Avoids disputes over partial refunds
