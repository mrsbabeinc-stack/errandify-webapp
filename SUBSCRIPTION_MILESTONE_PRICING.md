# Milestone Rewards & Adjusted Pricing Analysis

## What Are Milestone Rewards?

Milestone rewards are **bonus ad credits** that users earn when they hit activity goals.

Example:
- User posts 50 tasks → System gives them $20 extra ad credit
- User posts 100 tasks → System gives them $50 extra ad credit

**These are NOT separate rewards - they're ADDED TO their monthly ad credit.**

### How It Works:

**GOLD ($79/month example)**

Monthly allocation:
```
Base ad credit: $200/month (resets every month)
Milestone reward: $20 (one-time when they hit 50 tasks)
Milestone reward: $50 (one-time when they hit 100 tasks)
─────────────────────────────────────────
Total available in year: $200×12 + $20 + $50 = $2,470
```
