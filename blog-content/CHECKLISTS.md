# PUBLICATION CHECKLISTS
*Use before publishing any article*

---

## VIRAL HOOK CHECKLIST (For Growth & Trending Articles)

**Use this for all articles with real people + stories**

### Headline

- [ ] **Specific + emotional hook**
  - ❌ Bad: "Working Mothers Tips"
  - ✅ Good: "She Negotiated 3 Days/Week—Here's Exactly What Happened"

- [ ] **Numbers work**
  - ❌ Bad: "How to earn money"
  - ✅ Good: "How She Earned $3,000/month (Timeline + breakdown)"

- [ ] **Makes people curious**
  - Read headline aloud: "Would I click this?"
  - If no: revise

### Opening (First 100 words)

- [ ] **Introduce person immediately**
  - Name (real or anonymous) + brief context
  - ✅ "Meet Sarah, a 35-year-old marketing manager who just returned from maternity leave"
  
- [ ] **State the challenge clearly**
  - What was the problem?
  - ✅ "Childcare cost $1,200/month—nearly her entire take-home"

- [ ] **Promise the payoff**
  - What will reader learn?
  - ✅ "Here's how she saved $600/month using a government program most people don't know about"

### Body Content

- [ ] **Real quotes from the person**
  - At least 5 direct quotes
  - Captures emotion + personality
  - Example: "When approval came through, I just cried"

- [ ] **Specific numbers (if comfortable sharing)**
  - Income: [actual if they allow]
  - Costs: [before/after]
  - Timeline: [exact months]
  - ✅ "Took 2 weeks to research, 10 days for approval"

- [ ] **Emotional arc**
  - Problem → struggle → turning point → solution → outcome
  - Reader feels journey, not just reads steps

- [ ] **Honest challenges included**
  - NOT: "Everything worked perfectly"
  - ✅: "Month 2 was harder, here's why..."
  - NO overpromising

- [ ] **Real timeline (not compressed)**
  - ✅ "8 months to earn back $5,000"
  - NOT: "Quick tips to earn $5,000"

### Ending

- [ ] **What she'd tell others**
  - Direct advice from her perspective
  - Realistic, not preachy
  - ✅ "Look for programs you might qualify for—you don't know until you try"

- [ ] **Call to action (soft)**
  - NOT: "Sign up for Errandify" (premature)
  - ✅: "Share your story in comments if you relate"
  - OR: "What's your approach? Tell us below"

### Shareability

- [ ] **Pull-out quotes identified**
  - 2-3 best quotes highlighted for social sharing
  - Example: "When approval came through, I just cried"

- [ ] **No jargon**
  - Read aloud: Are there words your mom wouldn't understand?
  - Replace with simple words

- [ ] **Relatable to wide audience**
  - Not just for people in exact situation
  - ✅ Childcare subsidy story helps all working parents
  - NOT: Just for women with infants

### Legal/Safety

- [ ] **Permission confirmed**
  - [ ] Use name? (if yes, spelling correct)
  - [ ] Use photo? (if yes, file attached)
  - [ ] Anonymize? (if yes, changed all identifying details)
  - [ ] Any details to remove? (confirmed)

- [ ] **No overpromising**
  - ✅ "Here's what worked for her"
  - NOT: "You can do this too"
  - ✅ "Took 8 months"
  - NOT: "Quick fix"

## LEGAL SAFETY CHECKLIST (All Articles)

**Run through EVERY article before publishing**

### Data & Sources

- [ ] **Every statistic has a source**
  - Cite government websites when possible
  - Link directly (not paraphrased)
  - Example: "According to ECDA [link], childcare subsidy rates are..."

- [ ] **All sources are credible**
  - Government > News > Blog
  - NOT: Twitter/Reddit as main source
  - If using non-gov source: verified independently

- [ ] **Data is current**
  - Within last 6 months (ideally)
  - If older: state why: "Latest available data as of [DATE]"
  - Flag if rates have changed: "Note: These rates changed [DATE]"

- [ ] **Numbers are specific, not vague**
  - ✅ "S$600/month"
  - NOT: "around $600"
  - ✅ "1,000+ women"
  - NOT: "many women"

### Language

- [ ] **No absolute claims**
  - ✅ "May help"
  - NOT: "Will solve"
  - ✅ "One approach that worked"
  - NOT: "The solution"

- [ ] **No guarantees**
  - ✅ "Timeline: 3-6 months for most"
  - NOT: "You'll find a job in 3 months"
  - ✅ "She earned $3,000/month"
  - NOT: "You can earn $3,000/month"

- [ ] **No medical/legal advice**
  - If article mentions health: "Consult a doctor"
  - If article mentions law: "Check with [authority]"
  - Not giving personal advice

- [ ] **Honest about limitations**
  - ✅ "This program helps, but gaps exist"
  - NOT: "This solves everything"
  - ✅ "Not everyone qualifies"
  - NOT: "Everyone benefits"

### Tone

- [ ] **Not selling Errandify**
  - Article is pure helpful content
  - NO: "Errandify makes this easier"
  - NO: Errandify product mentions
  - (Later, when launched, can reference naturally)

- [ ] **Not advocacy/criticism**
  - ✅ Neutral on government programs
  - NOT: "Government should do X"
  - ✅ "Here's what exists"
  - NOT: "It's not enough"

- [ ] **Respectful of sensitive topics**
  - If article mentions abuse/health: gentle tone
  - Validate experience
  - Link to actual resources
  - Not sensationalizing

### Format

- [ ] **Disclaimer section included**
  ```
  ═══════════════════════════════════════════
  IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER
  
  This information is current as of [DATE].
  Government policies and rates change.
  
  Always verify with official sources:
  [Link to official website]
  
  This is educational content, not legal or 
  financial advice.
  ═══════════════════════════════════════════
  ```

- [ ] **Publication/update date visible**
  - "Published: June 21, 2026"
  - "Last Updated: June 21, 2026"

- [ ] **Sources listed at bottom**
  - Direct links to all sources
  - Clickable + current
  - Verified just before publishing

### Real Stories

- [ ] **Permission form signed** (if applicable)
  - Name approval confirmed
  - Photo approval confirmed
  - Details to avoid confirmed
  - Anonymity choice clear

- [ ] **Numbers verified**
  - Asked for proof if relevant
  - ✅ "Can I see your approval letter?" (for subsidy story)
  - Document confirmed amounts
  - NOT making up numbers

- [ ] **No identifiable details**
  - If anonymous: no company name, exact suburb, specific dates
  - If named: okay to use these
  - Test: Could someone identify them from article?

- [ ] **Story is not overgeneralized**
  - ✅ "This is her story, individual results vary"
  - NOT: "This is how all mothers do it"
  - Clear: "One approach that worked for her"

### Sensitive Content

**If article mentions:**

- [ ] **Domestic violence/abuse**
  - [ ] Gentle, respectful tone
  - [ ] Link to actual support: PAVE, AWARE, helplines
  - [ ] Full anonymity offered & used
  - [ ] Not sensationalizing experience
  - [ ] Safety prioritized

- [ ] **Mental health/depression/anxiety**
  - [ ] Validate experience
  - [ ] Note: "Not medical advice, consult doctor"
  - [ ] Link to counseling services
  - [ ] Not diagnosing

- [ ] **Financial hardship/poverty**
  - [ ] Respectful language
  - [ ] Link to actual resources
  - [ ] Not pitying tone
  - [ ] Empowering not tragic

- [ ] **Discrimination/harassment**
  - [ ] Supportive tone
  - [ ] Link to recourse (HR, government)
  - [ ] Validate experience
  - [ ] Clear it's not their fault

### Final Read-Through

- [ ] **Read headline aloud**
  - Compelling?
  - Accurate?
  - No false promises?

- [ ] **Read opening paragraph**
  - Engaging?
  - Clear premise?
  - Sets up story well?

- [ ] **Skim body for:**
  - Specific examples (good)
  - Vague statements (remove)
  - Unsourced claims (add source)
  - Tone consistency (keep consistent)

- [ ] **Read ending**
  - Provides value?
  - Empowering not prescriptive?
  - Leaves door open for comments/questions?

- [ ] **Check every link**
  - [ ] Click each URL
  - [ ] Verify it works
  - [ ] Verify it's current (not redirected)

### Before Hitting Publish

- [ ] **Have you read it aloud?** (catches awkward phrasing)
- [ ] **Could a lawyer find issues?** (NO = good)
- [ ] **Could someone feel misled?** (NO = good)
- [ ] **Is every fact checkable?** (YES = good)
- [ ] **Would you be proud to share this?** (YES = publish)

## WEEKLY LEGAL AUDIT

**Every Friday: Check last week's published articles**

- [ ] All links still working?
- [ ] Any facts that need updating?
- [ ] Any reader comments raising concerns?
- [ ] Outdated rates/programs mentioned?
- [ ] Need to add "last updated" date?

**If yes to any:** Update article + add note: "Updated June 28, 2026: [What changed]"

## RED FLAGS (Don't Publish If Present)

🚩 **NEVER publish if:**

- [ ] Article is primarily selling Errandify
- [ ] Claims are unverified or made-up
- [ ] Overpromises results ("guaranteed to earn $X")
- [ ] Medical/legal advice without disclaimer
- [ ] Real person identified without permission
- [ ] Sensationalizes sensitive topics
- [ ] Government program data unverified
- [ ] Links to sources don't work
- [ ] Tone is advocacy (criticizing government/society)
- [ ] Feels misleading in any way

**If ANY red flag present:** Do NOT publish. Revise first.
