## COLOR LEGEND

**Status:**
- 🟢 **Ready to publish** - Written, approved, scheduled
- 🟡 **In progress** - Being written or waiting for story approval
- 🟠 **Planned** - Outline done, waiting for resources
- 🔴 **Blocked** - Waiting on something (story approval, data, etc.)
- ⚪ **Archived** - Old/replaced/not publishing

**Type:**
- **Foundation** - Reference articles (slow ranking, high authority)
- **Growth** - Real stories (viral potential, medium ranking)
- **Trending** - News-based + timely (quick ranking, high viral)
