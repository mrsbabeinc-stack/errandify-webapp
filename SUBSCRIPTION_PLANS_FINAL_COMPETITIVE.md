# Errandify Subscription Plans - Final Proposal

## Overview
Three-tier subscription model for **companies/service providers** with commission savings, exclusive features, and networking perks. Compared to individuals using Errandify free.

---

## 🚫 INDIVIDUAL (NO SUBSCRIPTION) - FREE

### Target User
Individual service providers, solo doers, casual users

### What You Get
- ✅ Post tasks & bid on tasks
- ✅ Earn 50-500 EP/month (from activity)
- ✅ Basic messaging
- ✅ Access to Hana AI (basic)
- ✅ Payment processing
- ✅ 1-5 star ratings

### Platform Commission
- **20% commission** on all transactions
- Example: SGD $100 task = keep SGD $80

### Limitations
- ❌ No team management
- ❌ No financial reports
- ❌ No API access
- ❌ No bulk operations
- ❌ No multi-location support
- ❌ No company branding/profile

### Use Case
Solo cleaner doing 5-10 tasks/month on their own

## ✅ Summary

### Why Subscriptions Work

**For Errandify:**
- Recurring revenue from subscriptions
- Lower churn (commission savings create stickiness)
- Tier progression (Silver → Gold → Platinum)

**For Users:**
- **Individuals:** Free version works great
- **Small teams (Silver):** Commission savings + team tools ($972/year net benefit)
- **Growing companies (Gold):** Financial insights + networking ($11,412/year net benefit)
- **Enterprises (Platinum):** Full business automation system ($118,812/year net benefit)

### The Magic Staircase
```
Individual (Free)
    ↓ outgrow solo
Silver ($19/month, 2-5 people)
    ↓ need visibility
Gold ($49/month, 6-15 people)
    ↓ scale to enterprise
Platinum ($99/month, 16+ people)
```

**Each tier has real system features that unlock at that scale, not artificial restrictions.**
