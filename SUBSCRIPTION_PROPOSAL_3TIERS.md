## 🎯 Ad Credits Explained

### What Are They?
Monthly advertising budget that companies spend **on Errandify** to promote their own tasks and services.

### How They Use It
- **Banner ads** (promote their profile)
- **In-feed ads** (boost task visibility)
- **Featured listings** (top placement)
- **Campaign promotions** (weekend specials)

### Example: Silver Company (SGD $50/month)
```
Week 1: Spend $50 on banner ad
        → Gets 5,000+ impressions for their profile

Week 3: New month arrives, gets fresh $50 credit
        → Can run in-feed ads or save for bigger campaign

Month 3: Accumulated credits or fresh allocation
        → Run major promotion for their services
```

### Why Ad Credits Are Better Than Cash EP Rewards
| Aspect | Ad Credits | Cash Rewards |
|--------|-----------|--------------|
| **Value to them** | Immediate visibility ✅ | Maybe use later ❌ |
| **Platform benefit** | Drives traffic ✅ | They leave platform ❌ |
| **Cost to you** | Minimal (server space) ✅ | SGD $0.10 per point lost ❌ |
| **Engagement** | Monthly (recurring) ✅ | One-time ❌ |
| **Profitability** | Profitable ✅ | Unprofitable ❌ |

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Will free users be upset about premium features?**
A: No. Free users get commission at 20% which is fair. Paid tiers unlock tools that only large teams need.

**Q: Can users downgrade?**
A: Yes. Pro-rating applies (e.g., downgrade from Platinum → Gold mid-month = refund difference).

**Q: What if they don't use all ad credits?**
A: Credits expire monthly. No rollover. Encourages monthly spending.

**Q: Can they use ad credits on external ads?**
A: No. Credits are **only** for Errandify platform ads. Keeps them on platform, keeps them engaged.

**Q: How do we compete with free users?**
A: Paid users get commission savings (immediate ROI) + tools that scale (team management, payroll, reporting).
