- ✅ 2-4% savings per transaction = real money motivation

- ✅ 2x/3x/5x rewards encourage activity
- ✅ Gamification drives platform usage
- ✅ Scales with tier (makes sense)

- ✅ SGD $50-500/month is real value
- ✅ Keeps engagement on platform (not external)
- ✅ Monthly resets = monthly logins

## ⚠️ ISSUES TO FIX

### Issue #1: Inconsistent Bonus Structure
**Problem:** Bonuses are different across tiers:
```
Silver:    $20 at 50 tasks ONLY
Gold:      $20 at 50, $50 at 100
Platinum:  $20 at 50, $50 at 100 (same as Gold?) ❌
```

**Why it's confusing:**
- Silver gets 1 milestone
- Gold gets 2 milestones
- Platinum gets... same 2 as Gold? That's backwards!

**Better approach:**
```
Silver:    $20 at 50 tasks
Gold:      $20 at 50, $50 at 100
Platinum:  $20 at 50, $50 at 100, $100 at 200 ✅
```

**Or even better (tiered by effort):**
```
Silver:    SGD $20 at 50 tasks
Gold:      SGD $50 at 50, SGD $100 at 100 
Platinum:  SGD $100 at 50, SGD $200 at 100, SGD $500 at 200
```

This shows escalating rewards for higher tiers.

---

### Issue #2: "Ad Credit" Terminology in Bonuses

**Current wording:**
> "Bonuses: $20 Ad Credit at 50 tasks posted"

**Problem:** Users might think the bonus is in limited "credits" - confusing with payment systems.

**Better wording:**
> "Milestone Reward: Earn SGD $20 bonus ad spend at 50 tasks posted"

Or simply:
> "Bonus Reward: SGD $20 extra ad budget when you post 50 tasks"

This clarifies it's **actual money value**, not a system credit.

---

### Issue #3: Missing Tier Differentiation in Features

**Current:**
```
Silver: Team coordination, AI dashboard
Gold:   All Silver + events + newsletter
Platinum: All Gold + blog + quarterly newsletter + priority events
```

**Problem:** Feature list feels thin for Gold/Platinum. Customers might ask: "What else am I paying for?"

**Better approach - add visible differentiation:**

**Silver (SGD $28):**
- Team coordination (up to 5)
- AI-powered dashboard
- Newsletter feature
- AI recommendations

**Gold (SGD $78) - "The Scaler":**
- All Silver features
- **Team management (up to 15)** ← Highlight this
- **Events invitations (networking)**
- **Top newsletter placement**
- **Milestone rewards**
- **Extra visibility boost**

**Platinum (SGD $148) - "The Dominator":**
- All Gold features
- **Unlimited team members**
- **Blog publishing feature**
- **Quarterly featured newsletter**
- **Priority event access**
- **Higher milestone rewards**
- **VIP community access**

See the difference? Make each tier feel like a real upgrade, not just "more of the same."

---

### Issue #4: Milestone Bonuses May Not Drive Behavior

**Current:** SGD $20-100 bonuses for posting 50-200 tasks

**Analysis:**
```
At SGD $5K/month revenue:
- User is earning SGD $4,100-4,400/month
- A SGD $20 bonus is 0.5% of monthly income
- Not very motivating ❌

Better: Milestone bonuses as percentage of ad credits:
Silver:  Post 50 tasks → Get 100% EXTRA ad budget (SGD $50 bonus)
Gold:    Post 50 tasks → Get 50% EXTRA ad budget (SGD $100 bonus)
Platinum: Post 50 tasks → Get 50% EXTRA ad budget (SGD $250 bonus)
```

This feels more impactful!

---

### Issue #5: Team Size Jumps Are Too Big

**Current:**
```
Silver: 5 people
Gold: 15 people (3x jump)
Platinum: Unlimited (infinite jump)
```

**Problem:** A company with 7 people can't use Silver (too small) but Gold (SGD $78) might feel expensive.

**Better option (but tricky):**
```
Silver: Up to 5 people
Gold: Up to 15 people
Platinum: 15+ people
```

This works, but consider: Do you want to upsell at 6 people or 15? Your current structure forces 6-person teams to jump 3x cost.

**Not a dealbreaker though** - just something to monitor during launch.

---

### Issue #6: Quarterly Newsletter for Platinum Only?

**Current structure:**
```
Gold: Newsletter feature
Platinum: Quarterly newsletter feature
```

**Problem:** What's the difference? "Quarterly" sounds like LESS, not more!

**Better positioning:**
```
Gold: Monthly newsletter feature
Platinum: Premium quarterly features + monthly newsletter
```

Or:
```
Gold: Newsletter feature (featured monthly)
Platinum: Newsletter + exclusive quarterly reports
```

Make Platinum feel like they're getting MORE, not less.

---

## 🎯 FINAL RECOMMENDATION

### Keep What's Working (90% of it)

✅ Pricing: SGD $28/$78/$148
✅ Commission: 18/17/16%
✅ Ad Credits: SGD $50/200/500
✅ EP Multiplier: 2x/3x/5x
✅ Team sizes: 5/15/Unlimited
✅ ROI positioning

### Fix These 3 Things

1. **Milestone bonuses:** Make Platinum's higher (SGD $20/50/100 → SGD $50/100/200)
2. **Terminology:** "Milestone Reward: Earn SGD $X" instead of "Ad Credit"
3. **Newsletter:** Clarify Platinum gets MORE newsletter features, not fewer
