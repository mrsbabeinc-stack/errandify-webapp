## 💰 EP Points Breakdown

### What are EP Points?
Errandify Points (EP) are a rewards currency that companies can use to:
- **Gift to team members** for performance/incentives
- **Reward contractors/partners** for referrals/loyalty
- **Run customer loyalty programs** to increase repeat business
- **Run seasonal promotions** and special campaigns

### Monthly Allowance by Tier

| Tier | EP/Month | Annual EP | Use Cases |
|------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Silver** | 500 | 6,000 | Small team bonuses, occasional rewards |
| **Gold** | 2,000 | 24,000 | Monthly team incentives, customer programs |
| **Platinum** | 10,000 | 120,000 | Large-scale reward programs, company-wide initiatives |

### Example: How Companies Use EP Points

**Silver Company (5 team members):**
- 500 EP/month = 100 EP per team member
- Award 50-100 EP for top performer of the month
- Give referral bonuses (50-100 EP)
- Run quarterly customer loyalty draw (200 EP prize pool)

**Gold Company (15 team members):**
- 2,000 EP/month = 133 EP per team member
- Weekly performance bonuses (50-100 EP each)
- Monthly top performer award (200 EP)
- Customer loyalty program (500 EP/month prize pool)
- Referral bonuses (100-200 EP per referral)

**Platinum Company (100+ team members):**
- 10,000 EP/month
- Monthly top performer per region (200 EP each = $2K value)
- Quarterly excellence awards (500 EP = $5K value)
- Large customer loyalty campaigns (2,000-5,000 EP/month)
- Staff referral program (100-500 EP per hire)
- Anniversary/milestone bonuses (100-1,000 EP each)

### Why This Works Better Than Ad Spend

**Ad Spend Limits:**
- Don't benefit most companies (only if they advertise)
- Creates friction (companies hit limit)
- Not aligned with business growth

**EP Points:**
- **Universal benefit** (every company can use for rewards)
- **Grows value** (bigger companies get more points)
- **Aligned with growth** (more revenue = more commission savings + more EP budget)
- **Sticky** (companies use points monthly, stay engaged)
