# Errandify Subscription - Growth & Usage Incentives

## Problem with Current Model
Current subscriptions offer commission savings + ad credits, but **don't directly incentivize heavy usage**.

We need subscriptions that make people **WANT to post more tasks and do more work on the platform**.

---

## 🎯 The Goal
**Solve the chicken-and-egg problem:**
- New app = low liquidity (few tasks available)
- Subscribers need incentives to POST MORE (attract doers)
- More posts = more visibility for doers to COMPLETE MORE
- More activity = network effects = platform growth

---

## 💡 NEW INCENTIVE STRUCTURE

### What Drives Activity?
1. **More visibility** (posts seen by more people)
2. **Faster task completion** (tasks get done quicker)
3. **Better quality matches** (better bidders)
4. **Higher earnings** (earn more per task)
5. **Recognition** (top performers get featured)
