## 💡 Analysis: Is Pricing Fair?

### EP Cost to Errandify

When you **give 500/2000/5000 EP** to a subscriber:

| Tier | EP Given/Year | Cost to You | Subscription Revenue | Margin |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Silver** | 6,000 EP (SGD $600) | $600 | $228 | -$372 (loss) |
| **Gold** | 24,000 EP (SGD $2,400) | $2,400 | $588 | -$1,812 (loss) |
| **Platinum** | 60,000 EP (SGD $6,000) | $6,000 | $1,188 | -$4,812 (loss) |

**Problem:** You're losing money on EP allowance alone!
