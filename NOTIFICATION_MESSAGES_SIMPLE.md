# Notification Messages: Happy, Warm, Kampung & Compact

**Tone Guidelines:**
- **Happy:** Celebrate wins, show enthusiasm, positive language
- **Warm:** Use names, human conversation, show you care
- **Kampung:** Community-friendly, neighborly, helpful, no fancy language
- **Compact:** 1-2 lines max, no fluff, crystal clear

---

## KEY PRINCIPLES FOR ALL MESSAGES

### 1. Use Names When Possible
WRONG: "Someone bid $50"
RIGHT: "John bid $50"

### 2. Include Numbers & Details
WRONG: "Payment released"
RIGHT: "$50 released to John"

### 3. Keep It Friendly & Natural
WRONG: "Action required"
RIGHT: "Please rate John within 48 hours"

### 4. Be Specific About What's Next
WRONG: "You need to rate"
RIGHT: "Rate John within 48 hours so he gets paid"

### 5. Celebrate Good News
WRONG: "5-star rating received"
RIGHT: "Sarah gave you 5 stars - 'Perfect work, very happy!' Great job!"

### 6. Show Timeframes Clearly
WRONG: "Confirmation needed"
RIGHT: "Confirm within 24 hours"

### 7. Stay Warm & Neighborly
WRONG: "Error: dispute has been filed"
RIGHT: "Oops - there's a disagreement here. Our team is checking it out"

### 8. Keep It Compact
WRONG: "The asker has requested that you make some changes to the work you completed because they are not completely satisfied with the quality."
RIGHT: "Sarah wants a small fix - let's make her happy"

---

## MESSAGE CHECKLIST (Every Message Must Pass)

- Uses positive, friendly language
- Includes specific names and amounts
- Feels like talking to a friend, not a robot
- Fits in 1-2 lines
- Crystal clear next action
- Shows respect for the person
- Community-friendly and neighborly
- No fancy jargon or complicated words
