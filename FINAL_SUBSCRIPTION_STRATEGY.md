# Final Subscription Strategy - Monthly Lock-In + Annual Plans

## 🎯 THE STRATEGY: Smart Lock-In at Both Levels

### MONTHLY: Can Only Cancel At End of Month
- Prevents grab-and-go abuse ✅
- Still provides flexibility ✅
- Charges full month upfront ✅

### ANNUAL: Can Only Cancel After 30 Days (Then Pro-Rata)
- Longer commitment = stickier ✅
- Better revenue guaranteed ✅
- Users think twice before canceling ✅

This SOLVES all edge cases without complexity!

## ✅ HOW THIS HANDLES EDGE CASES

### Edge Case #1: User Upgrades Mid-Month Then Wants Out

**Scenario:**
```
User on Silver Monthly (SGD $28, charged June 1)
Upgrades to Gold Monthly (SGD $78) on June 15
Wants to cancel June 20
```

**With your rule:**
```
✅ Can cancel, but takes effect June 30
✅ Still charged for full Gold month (SGD $78)
✅ No refund for partial use
✅ Next month reverts to Silver
✅ Grab-and-go prevented ✅

User learns: Don't upgrade mid-month unless you're sure
Platform learns: Full month revenue guaranteed
```

### Edge Case #2: Grab Annual Benefits Then Cancel

**Scenario:**
```
User commits to Platinum Annual (SGD $1,420)

Wants to cancel after 1 week
```

**With your rule:**
```

✅ First 30 days: Non-refundable
✅ After 30 days: Pro-rata refund = (SGD $1,420 ÷ 365 days × days remaining)
✅ Example: Cancel on day 15 = $0 refund (within 30 days)
✅ Example: Cancel on day 120 = SGD $1,080 refund
✅ Abuse prevented ✅
```

## 🎯 POLICY CLARITY

### Monthly Subscription Policy

```
BILLING:
- Charges on same day each month
- Full month charged upfront (not pro-rated)
- No refunds during active month

UPGRADING:
- Takes effect immediately
- Pay difference to remaining days × new rate
- Example: Upgrade to Gold on June 15 (16 days left in June)
  Old rate: SGD $28 ÷ 30 days = $0.93/day × 16 = $14.88
  New rate: SGD $78 ÷ 30 days = $2.60/day × 16 = $41.60
  Charge: $41.60 - $14.88 = $27 (difference)
  Total June bill: $28 (old) + $27 (diff) = $55

DOWNGRADING:
- Same logic: Pay difference
- Example: Downgrade to Silver on June 15
  Refund: $14.88 credit toward next month
  Note: If credit > new rate, applies to next month

CANCELING:
- Can cancel anytime
- But cancellation takes effect at END OF CURRENT MONTH
- Last month is charged in full
- No refunds during month

EXAMPLE CANCELLATION:
- Cancel on June 15
- June 1-30 still charged (SGD $28)
- Cancellation effective July 1
- July charge does NOT happen
```

---

### Annual Subscription Policy

```
BILLING:
- One charge, paid upfront for 12 months
- 20% discount off monthly rates
- Renewal date is exactly 12 months later

UPGRADING:
- Can upgrade anytime
- Pay difference for remaining months at annual rate
- Example: Platinum Annual (SGD $1,420) → Want Gold Annual
  Remaining: 9 months (Sept 15 - June 15)
  Gold annual rate: SGD $748 ÷ 12 = $62.33/month
  Refund: $62.33/month × 9 = $560.97
  User receives: $560.97 credit toward next year

DOWNGRADING:
- Can request anytime
- Option A: Free months (stay on higher tier until auto-switch)
- Option B: Immediate downgrade with service credit
- Option C: Cancel with pro-rata refund

CANCELING:
- Within 30 days: 100% refund (no questions asked)
- After 30 days: Pro-rata refund for remaining days
- Example: Cancel on day 120 of 365
  Remaining: 245 days
  Refund: (SGD $1,420 ÷ 365) × 245 = $950.68

EXAMPLE CANCELLATION:
- Subscribe June 15, 2026
- Renewal: June 15, 2027
- Cancel on Sept 15, 2026 (92 days)
- Remaining days: 273 (Sept 15 2026 - June 15 2027)
- Refund: (SGD $1,420 ÷ 365) × 273 = $1,061
```
